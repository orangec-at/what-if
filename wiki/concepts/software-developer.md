# Software Developer

## Summary

A software developer is the primary user of this ontology: a knowledge worker who designs, builds, tests, reviews, ships, and maintains software systems.

## Why it matters

The ontology should optimize for developer decisions: what to learn, what to build, what to test, what to automate, and what tradeoffs to document.

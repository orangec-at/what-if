# Knowledge Compounding

## Summary

Knowledge compounding means each source, answer, and correction improves the persistent wiki rather than disappearing into chat history.

## Why it matters

For software developers, compounding turns repeated learning and debugging into reusable context: decisions, patterns, tradeoffs, and source-backed claims become easier to find and connect.
